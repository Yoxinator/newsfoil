About Newsfoil.com

This is the future site for newsfoil.com. Newsfoil is a news site that is run by its users. Users will be able to create, read and rate news articles. Users will use their "influence" to increase how many people read an article. In other words, the users are the "editors" deciding which article hit the front page and which articles are relegated to the back page. Check back in a few weeks and we should have our site up and running.

This site is a school project created by Mark, Larry, Dave and Alton.
